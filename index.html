About Me

A programmer since the age of 14. I first discovered the wonders of software development when I got in touch with my first game development platform back in 1997. Since then, development has been my life and I realized that a lot of my creative impulses could be satisfied with software development. At 18, I was already working as a software developer and studying Computer Science and from then on I've been on this amazing journey of discovery and accomplishment that technology provides.

Who am I ? I'm a computer scientist with multiple personalities, the IT specialist, the Project Manager and the 3D/game/app hobbyist developer. All those personalities have in common one thing: Technology. Technology is the way through which I can materialize ideas and change the world around me. Working with technology is what allowed me to discover the meaning of the word purpose.
